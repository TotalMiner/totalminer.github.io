## hail

### Add hail.

Spec:
```lua
hail(
	x,
	y,
	z,
	radius,
	duration,
	intensity,
	r,
	g,
	b,
	size_x,
	size_z)
```
## Parameters:
- `x:` The x component of the position of the center of the hail storm.
- `y:` The y component of the position of the center of the hail storm.
- `z:` The z component of the position of the center of the hail storm.
