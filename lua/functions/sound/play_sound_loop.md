
# (Official) LUA Scripting Documentation

## play_sound_loop

### Play a looped sound at a voxel position.

Spec:
```lua
play_sound_loop(
	name,
	x,
	y,
	z,
	loop,
	delay,
	count,
	volume,
	range)
```
## Parameters:
- `name:` 
- `x:` The x component of the position.
- `y:` The y component of the position.
- `z:` The z component of the position.
- `loop:` 
- `delay:` 
- `count:`
